Plastic Limt Test

Inroduction Experimental Setup Procedure Data Analysis Result

Aim:-

To determine the plastic limit of the soil sample and to calculate plasticity index, Toughness index of fine-grained soil.

Theory :-

Determination of Plastic Limit is as important as Liquid Limit so as to ascertain Plasticity Index, Ip of the soil. The plastic limit of a soil is the moisture content, expressed as a percentage of the weight of the oven-dry soil, at the boundary between the plastic and semi-solid states of consistency.
